  • Ferdinand Vogler: Getting Web-Typography just right with SASS

    “95% of the information on the web is written language” (Oliver Reichenstein). Striving for typographic structure, hierachy and legibility across various devices should be a must in every designers skillset. In this speech I want to show you the basics of well-set type and how SASS can be a powerful ally to achieve that.
